Weiliang Chen | 陈伟亮

I'm a first-year PhD student in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University , advised by Prof. Yueqi Duan and Prof. Jiwen Lu . In 2024, I received dual Bachelor’s degrees in science and engineering from Weiyang College, Tsinghua University..

My research interest lies in Computer Vision (e.g., 3D AIGC and Video Generation) and Autonomous Driving.

If you are interested in working with us (in person or remotely) as an intern at Tsinghua University, please feel free to drop me an email.
